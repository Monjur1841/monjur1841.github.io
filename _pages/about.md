Welcome to my academic portfolio! 

I am an Economist, Researcher, and Corporate Professional currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Economics at **North South University**, Dhaka. I hold a BBA in Economics & Finance (Cum Laude) from the same institution.

My research interests primarily lie at the intersection of **Development Economics, Financial Econometrics, and Climate Policy**. I am particularly passionate about using advanced data analysis (Python, R, Stata) to forecast financial markets and evaluate socioeconomic resilience in developing nations.

Currently, I work as an **Executive in Corporate & Regulatory Affairs** at US-Bangla Assets Ltd., where I manage regulatory compliance for 38 subsidiary companies. Alongside my corporate role, I serve as an **Economics Research Assistant**, co-authoring peer-reviewed publications in journals such as *F1000Research* and the *Canadian Journal of Business & Information Studies*.

I am always open to collaborative research opportunities, policy discussions, and roles within economic think tanks and development agencies.
